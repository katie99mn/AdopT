Shu Lan Schaut

Hi! My name is Shu Lan and I am a junior studying Neurobiology and English Literature. In my free time, I love to bake, read (fiction preferably!), and spend time with my friends. I also like to try and explore more of Madison whenever I can, from trying new restaurants to enjoying the outdoors.

I was adopted from Fencheng in the Jiangxi province of China when I was 14 months old and grew up in Kenosha, WI. Growing up in the Midwest as a person of color can be pretty isolating at times, even more so as a transracial adoptee in a White family. As a transracial adoptee, at times it feels like you are a part of separate two worlds, yet are not truly belonging in either. AdopT has created an opportunity for me to connect with other people who have felt the same and can relate to that unique perspective. In my role in AdopT, I hope I can help fellow transracial adoptees feel a greater sense of belonging at UW.
