We're so glad you're here!

Transracial Adoptees at UW-Madison is a new student organization dedicated to supporting transracial adoptees (and other intersecting identities) to find a sense of community and belonging on campus!

Our Mission

Find & Build Community.


AdopT. is an organization that aims to create a safe and welcoming environment for adoptees and intersecting identities to share their experiences and join in community. We strive to provide a space where members can engage in social, educational, and service activities pertaining to adoption while promoting multicultural spaces on campus.
