Grace Drayton

Hello!

I’m Grace Drayton, currently a senior at the University of Wisconsin – Madison pursuing a Bachelors of Business Administration in Management and Human Resources along with a certificate in Asian American Studies. A few of my passions and hobbies include exploring and experimenting to cook recipes from various global cuisines, running outdoors, and traveling.

Establishing AdopT. Club at UW–Madison, along with the leadership team, has been a truly special and fulfilling endeavor. I was born in Fuzhou, a city in southeastern Jiangxi province, China. I was adopted at 18 months and brought to the United States, where I grew up in a suburb of Minneapolis, Minnesota. I grew up in a bicultural household, my mom being born and raised in Argentina and my dad Fargo, North Dakota. Before they adopted me, my mom had my two older brothers. Since attending university, I’ve explored and learned more about my Asian American identity, something I was unfamiliar with growing up. I am excited to build a community of adoptees at UW–Madison and throughout the greater Madison area, while advocating for and empowering individuals with the unique adoptee identity.
